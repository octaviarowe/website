Meat is the idea that sprang forth from a crack in the mind of night mother, club rat, and DJ zongeh. Meat is all about showing the horrific insides that you refuse to put away for the sake of good taste. Meat is grinding your teeth until you find the root of the anxiety. Meat is what we find beneath the skin. Meat is what we come from and how we shape it is all the fashion. Meat is a taste that never leaves your mind.
